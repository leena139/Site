1Earth

The year is 2339 CE. Humans have been extinct for over 200 years due to a nuclear holocaust As the dust settles down in this post apocalyptic world , an evolved life form has been discovered Flopo, the nuclearly activated plants.

The Flopos have slowly evolved over the past 200 years and have started walking on Earth, surviving on the most valuable remaining resource on Earth clean water.

1Earth is a virtual reality, crypto collectibles gaming platform. Buy, breed, and nurture Flopos, unique warrior plants, to explore, build, and dominate the new world!

1Earth utilizes smart contracts to develop the next generation of digital game assets. Enabling players to buy, trade, and sell the game assets in a free market economy. Players can also create, share, and monetize their own original content and gameplays.
